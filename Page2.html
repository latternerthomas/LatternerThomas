SPORT

I enjoy all types of sports, whether team or individual. I really enjoy this world, but my favorite is still soccer.

I've been playing soccer since I was 8 but unfortunately this year I've had to stop because of my long-distance studies which take me from Blois to my club ASC.

Time with friends

What could be better than a little evening or afternoon with friends to make you happy, to tell each other your life and your stories.

The most important thing is to have a good time and get your energy back.

The world of cinema

I don't mind getting up on Sunday morning to see a good film in the cinema.

When it comes to film categories, I don't have any big preferences, I'm interested in everything.

But I lean more towards action, adventure and science fiction films.
